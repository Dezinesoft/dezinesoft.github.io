About

Affordable Rentals is a two-tier property rental and investment company. On the first level, we provide affordable living arrangements for families in South Florida through an array of apartments, condominiums, duplexes, triplexes, manufactured homes, studios, and efficiencies. On the second level we invest in residential and commercial properties: buildings, complexes, units, and other estates. Our number one priority is to get you moving in the right direction.

Founder

Affordable Rentals was founded by a man of modest means. A man of humble beginnings. A man who understood the value of ambition, hard work and drive. He grew up on the rough streets of New Jersey, and it was that same rugged lifestyle that helped mold him into the man he is today. Raised by a hard working single mother with no support from a father figure, he quickly evolved into a self-educated entrepreneur through life experiences. To earn extra income, he began selling shoes as an adolescent to anyone who would buy them. Shoes, however, were just the stepping stones to his success. It was his unwavering commitment and dedication to succeed, that catapulted his selling career from reeboks to real estate. Having served more than 30 years as an honorable Deputy Sherriff for Broward County, FL, he served not only his tenants but also their communities. He’s always had a passion to protect, serve, and give back. Today, Garry Martin owns rental properties all throughout Broward County, FL and makes living affordable for the average family. Outside of business, he enjoys spending quality time with his children, grandchildren, girlfriend and apprentice. His hobbies include stepping (dancing), playing chess, traveling to exotic countries, fine dining, watching movies, trying new things and learning about different cultures. He also has a very strong faith in the Lord and makes every effort to give back to the community. Whether he’s helping an individual on the side of the road, educating today’s youth with timeless wisdom, or housing a financially struggling individual, he always makes time to lend a helping hand. He is truly one of a kind.
