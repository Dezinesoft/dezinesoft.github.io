About

Affordable Rentals is a two-tier property rental and investment company. On one level, we provide affordable living arrangements for families in South Florida through an array of apartments, condominiums, duplexes, triplexes, manufactured homes, studios, and efficiencies. On the second level we invest in residential and commercial properties: buildings, complexes, units, and other estates. Our number one priority is to help you move in the right direction.

Founder

Affordable Rentals was founded by a man of humble upbringings. An ambitious, hard working New Jersey native with a heart of gold, life for him wasn’t always easy. Raised by a single mother with no direction or male guidance in life, he learned early on the importance of hard work. A self-taught entrepreneur, he began selling shoes as a kid to earn extra income. Shoes were only the stepping stones for his future. Through an unwavering commitment and dedication to succeed, he went from selling reebok to real estate. Throughout his 30-year career, he served as an honorable Deputy Sherriff for Broward County, FL helping keep the community safe and building his business on the side. It started with one investment. His first property. Scraping up the funds to make that first purchase was one of the toughest things he’s ever had to do. But, he often says, “if you can do a thing once, you can do it twice. If you can do it twice, you can do it thrice!” Not only has he done it thrice, he now owns rental properties all throughout Broward County and makes it affordable for the average family.
Outside of business, he enjoys spending quality time with his children, grandchildren, girlfriend and apprentice. His hobbies include stepping (dancing), playing chess, traveling to exotic countries, fine dining, watching movies, trying new things and learning about different cultures. He also has a very strong faith in the Lord and makes every effort to give back. Whether he’s helping an individual on the side of the road, educating today’s youth with timeless wisdom, or housing a financially struggling individual, he always makes time to give back. He is one of a kind.
